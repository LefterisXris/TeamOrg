TeamOrg Keep in touch with your team!! Having a team with multiple members, may be challenging. With TeamOrg you can have your team organized and keep in touch with all its members. Navigate to the Users section, to view, edit or add users on your team. Each use can be assigned to a role and be manageable in high level through its role. To view, edit or add roles, navigate to the Roles section. Check the activity of your team, from Events section. There you can check all the available events or posts of any member of your team.
